How to Use AuratBot

Welcome to AuratBot, your friendly AI companion! Here's a simple guide on how to use AuratBot:

Step 1: Start a Conversation

Click the "Start Chat" button to begin your conversation with AuratBot. It's like talking to a friend!

Step 2: Ask Questions

Feel free to ask any questions you have. AuratBot is here to help you with information on various topics like marriage, legal rights, health, and more.

Step 3: Engage in Conversations

Engage in conversations with AuratBot to seek guidance, information, or just to chat. It's designed to provide a safe and inclusive space for you.

Step 4: Explore Modules

Check out the different modules available to explore specific topics. You can find modules related to various aspects of life and get detailed information.

Step 5: Proceed to Chatbot

If you want to dive deeper or have a more interactive experience, click the "Proceed to Chatbot" button to continue your conversation in the chatbot interface.

Remember, AuratBot is here to empower you with information and support. Enjoy your journey of empowerment!
